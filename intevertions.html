Our Interventions

At Ayarhep, interventions are practical actions that address urgent community challenges. From health to education, we act where the need is greatest to create lasting change.

Key Focus Areas

Health

Mobile clinics and health awareness programs to improve access to essential medical care.

Adolescent & Youth Sexual and Reproductive Health

Access to age-appropriate sexuality education.

HIV & STI Prevention and Treatment

Education and prevention programs targeting key populations (MSM, sex workers, people who inject drugs).

Women Empowerment

Vocational training and entrepreneurship support for women-led households.

Gender-Based Violence & Protection

Preventing and responding to sexual and gender-based violence (SGBV).

Youth Development

Skill-building workshops and sports programs fostering leadership and resilience.
