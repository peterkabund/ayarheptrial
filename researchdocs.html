Research, Documentation and Advocacy

Research, Documentation and Advocacy

Objective 2. To Increase support for Advocacy by AYPLHIV and affected communities in all aspects of decision-making in order to ensure access to optimal HIV/SRH services.

Main Activities

• Trainings on Data Collection;
Conducting Surveys & Mapping;
Managing and Analyzing Data;
Documenting Research Results & Best Practice;
Risk assessment outreaches – mobilization and sensitization
• Trainings on Advocacy; conducting Lobbying & Dialogue Meetings; Media Outreaches; Activism, Campaigns & Information provision
• Conduct Policy analysis & advocacy for policy change

Implementers and Stakeholders

Community
Opinion Leaders and Influencers
CBOs & NGOs

Outcomes

A strong movement of AYPLHIV in place & empowered, and is actively agitating for progressive national HIV/SRH policies that create an enabling environment for AYPLHIV access to HIV/SRH services without stigma and discrimination.
